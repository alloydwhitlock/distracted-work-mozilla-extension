Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The Short Version

We don't collect anything. Nothing. Zero. Zilch.

Website (distracted.work)

The distracted.work website is a static page. It does not:

  • Use cookies (except a local theme preference stored in your browser's localStorage)
  • Track your visits
  • Use any analytics services
  • Collect any personal information
  • Send any data to any server

Browser Extension (Distracted Work)

The Distracted Work browser extension operates entirely within your browser. It does not:

  • Collect or transmit any browsing data
  • Send your blocklist or settings to any server
  • Track which sites you visit or block
  • Phone home or communicate with any external service
  • Use any analytics or telemetry

All extension data (your blocked sites list, schedule settings, and preferences) is stored locally in your browser using the browser's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Data Storage

The only data stored is:

  • Website: A theme preference (light/dark) in your browser's localStorage.
  • Extension: Your blocked sites list, schedule settings, and preferences in the browser's local extension storage. You can clear this at any time by removing the extension.

Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any data from anyone, we don't collect data from children either.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page. But honestly, "we collect nothing" is a hard policy to change.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at privacy@distracted.work.
